Recipe of the Day: Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • Butter, Spices, Cream

Steps

  1. Saute onion & tomatoes
  2. Add spices & blend
  3. Add paneer & cream
  4. Serve hot
